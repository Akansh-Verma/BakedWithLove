Menu

Baked With Love offers a wide variety of freshly baked goods, including artisan breads, delicious pastries, and homemade cakes. Our bread selection includes sourdough, whole wheat, and rye, all made from high-quality, locally sourced ingredients. Our pastries range from classic croissants to more unique creations like matcha green tea danishes. We also have a wide selection of cakes for any occasion, including our signature chocolate cake, lemon lavender cake and gluten-free options. All our products are made from scratch daily, using only the freshest ingredients. Stop by today and treat yourself to something delicious!
